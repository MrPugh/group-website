Cross-cutting Research

Achieving challenging research visions demands expertise in many research fields. Our group fosters collaborations among researchers with varied backgrounds. A positive feedback loop where practical problems inspire theoretical investigation and where formal solutions are applied to solve real-world challenges.

Advanced Education

We are strongly involved in bringing bleeding-edge research topics to students and industrial teams. BSc graduates can apply to the MSc package in Digital Organisations: Models and Systems at SDU.

Industrial Collaboration

We seek strong synergies with industry. Industrial collaborations drive the investigation of new theories and the creation of new software tools to increase production efficiency.
